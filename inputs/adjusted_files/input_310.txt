However, this integration is not without its complexities. Global trends highlight the uneven distribution of AI benefits and risks across nations, raising crucial questions about equitable access and responsible deployment.  From a policy perspective, the ethical considerations surrounding AI, such as data privacy, algorithmic transparency, and accountability, demand careful consideration and robust regulatory frameworks.
