The "black box" nature of some AI algorithms can hinder their adoption, as clinicians need to be confident in the reasoning behind the AI's recommendations. However, the opportunities are significant. AI can assist dermatologists in managing their workload, providing faster and more efficient diagnoses, particularly in high-volume settings.
