AI also plays a crucial role in procedural generation, enabling the creation of vast and varied game worlds without requiring extensive manual design. This allows developers to create games with significantly greater scale and complexity than previously possible.  Moreover, AI is being increasingly utilized to create more realistic and believable non-player characters (NPCs), enhancing the immersion and emotional connection players have with the game world.  These advancements, however, raise concerns about the potential for AI to inadvertently create biased or unfair gameplay experiences.
