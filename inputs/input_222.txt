This increased transparency can build confidence in the use of AI in clinical settings and foster collaboration between humans and machines.  Finally, the integration of AI in radiology will likely lead to changes in the roles and responsibilities of radiologists.   Instead of simply interpreting images, radiologists may become more involved in managing the AI systems, overseeing quality control, and providing expert clinical judgment in complex cases.
